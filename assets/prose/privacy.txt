# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** October 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how VibeScript ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our websites at https://vibescript.online and https://builder.vibescript.online (collectively, the "Services"). By accessing or using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account, use our Services, or communicate with us, we may collect:

- **Account Information:** Email address, password (encrypted), and display name
- **Authentication Data:** Google account information if you sign in via Google OAuth
- **Payment Information:** Billing details processed through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)
- **Voice Input:** Audio recordings of your voice commands used to generate websites (processed in real-time and not permanently stored unless you save your project)
- **Content You Create:** Website designs, text, images, code, and other materials you generate or upload through our AI-powered builder
- **Communications:** Messages you send to us via email or support channels
- **Waitlist Information:** Email addresses submitted for beta access invitations

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect:

- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, generation requests, pages visited, time spent on the platform
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, IP address
- **Performance Data:** Error logs, load times, API response metrics
- **Security Data:** Cloudflare Turnstile verification tokens and anti-abuse signals
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** Session identifiers, authentication tokens, and preference settings

### 1.3 Information From Third Parties

We receive information from:

- **Supabase:** Authentication and session management data
- **Stripe:** Payment processing and transaction records
- **Google:** Profile information when you use Google sign-in
- **Cloudflare:** Security analysis and traffic protection data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Services
- Process your voice and text inputs to generate websites using AI
- Create, host, and manage your website projects
- Authenticate your identity and manage your account
- Process payments, subscriptions, and one-time exports
- Send transactional emails (confirmations, receipts, account notifications) via Resend
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
- Analyze usage patterns to improve performance and features
- Respond to your support requests and communications
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms and Conditions
- Communicate important updates about the Services (you may opt out of non-essential communications)

## 3. AI and Voice Processing

### 3.1 Voice Data

When you use voice input to describe your website:

- Audio is transmitted securely to our AI processing systems
- Voice data is converted to text and interpreted to generate website code
- Audio recordings are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers unless you save the project
- Voice data is never used to train third-party AI models
- We do not sell or share your voice recordings with third parties for advertising or marketing

### 3.2 AI Training

Our AI models are trained exclusively on:

- Publicly available, licensed, and allow-listed datasets
- Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns (not individual user content)
- No private user content, personal data, or proprietary designs are used for AI training without explicit consent

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party providers who perform services on our behalf:

- **Cloudflare:** Infrastructure hosting, security, CDN, and anti-DDoS protection
- **Supabase:** Authentication and database management
- **Stripe:** Payment processing and billing management
- **Resend:** Transactional email delivery
- **Google:** OAuth authentication services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

- Comply with legal processes, court orders, or government requests
- Enforce our Terms and Conditions or other agreements
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of VibeScript, our users, or the public
- Detect, prevent, or investigate fraud, security issues, or illegal activity

### 4.3 Business Transfers

If VibeScript is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 4.4 With Your Consent

We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy:

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion to comply with legal obligations
- **Payment Records:** Retained as required by tax and financial regulations (typically 7 years)
- **Voice Inputs:** Processed in real-time; not permanently stored unless part of a saved project
- **Website Projects:** Retained until you delete them or close your account
- **Logs and Analytics:** Typically retained for 90 days unless needed for security investigations

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at contact@vibescript.online.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### 6.1 Access and Portability

You can access your account information through the builder dashboard. You may request a copy of your personal data in a portable format.

### 6.2 Correction

You can update your account information directly in your account settings.

### 6.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and personal data. Note that we may retain certain information as required by law or legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, financial records).

### 6.4 Opt-Out

You can opt out of non-essential marketing communications by following unsubscribe links in emails. You cannot opt out of essential transactional messages related to your account and purchases.

### 6.5 Do Not Track

We do not respond to Do Not Track browser signals. We use minimal analytics for security and performance purposes only.

### 6.6 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights including:

- Right to object to processing
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
- Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

### 6.7 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@vibescript.online.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies and similar technologies to:

- Maintain your login session
- Remember your preferences
- Provide security through Cloudflare Turnstile
- Analyze performance and usage patterns

### 7.1 Types of Cookies

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for authentication and core functionality
- **Performance Cookies:** Help us understand how you use the Services
- **Security Cookies:** Protect against fraud and abuse

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies may impair functionality.

## 8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- Secure authentication via Supabase with password hashing
- Cloudflare security protections including DDoS mitigation and bot detection
- Regular security assessments and monitoring
- Access controls limiting employee and contractor access to personal data

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

These jurisdictions may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

By using the Services, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other jurisdictions. We implement appropriate safeguards, including standard contractual clauses, to protect your information during international transfers.
